About me

My name is Angel Rivera, I have a degree in mechanical engineering with a specialization in engineering 4.0, I have a master's degree in engineering, and I work as a researcher.

I am currently working on research to restart the flow of paraffinic oil on Petrobras' offshore platforms. The research results aim to understand the flow restart process and provide reliable data due to the difficulties in obtaining an accurate flow restart pressure value. Using data science to collect, manipulate, visualize and extract information from large volumes of data, it has been possible to help Petrobras in the calculations that avoid the oversizing of its exploration and production facilities, which is reflected in unnecessary expenses that can make the implementation of projects.

Observing at what the information provided by the data can do, I am looking for an opportunity to work professionally as a data scientist to improve business decision-making through solutions using data.
